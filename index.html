Our Story

Memorable stops along our journey together

Our First Date
Atlanta, GA
Dec 10, 2020

Matching on Hinge was the easy bit. Picking out the right outfit for our first date at Cypress Pint & Plate in Midtown Atlanta was a little more challenging. I ended up going with jeans and a buttondown. Classic. She ordered wings and fries. I ordered pizza eggrolls. It was love at first bite!
-Noah

Our First Trip
Cancun, MX
April 13, 2021

You learn a lot about someone when you travel together. When Noah and I decided to go on our first trip with his family, I knew it’d be make or break. His efficiency through airport parking/security/boarding and love of Sky Clubs definitely caught my attention but when we got back to Atlanta and I wasn’t ready to rush to my house, I knew this was something special.
-Ashley

Our First Family Reunion
Berea, KY
July 6, 2021

Meeting all of what seemed to be like 96 of Ashley's extended family over a 5-day annual reunion in Kentucky seemed like a good idea when I agreed to it months before. I started out strong, but I admit by day 3 I was fading... fast. Thankfully, I get a chance to redeem myself this July in... (checks notes) Missouri? Well, alright then. Wish me luck!
-Noah

Our (second) First Date
Memphis, TN
May 31, 2022

Noah and I were on a break when my mom passed away unexpectedly in May 2022. He was the first person I wanted to call and the person I needed by my side. We had our (second) first date eating pizza at a Costco in Memphis. It was full of a lot of tears (and a lot of pizza grease) and also a lot of the hugs I missed so much!
-Ashley

The Proposal
Atlanta, GA
February 14, 2023

My friends told me proposing on Valentine's Day would be too cliché, but I didn't see anyone else proposing that night at Rumi's Kitchen. So... We had a small table in the corner (not near the restroom OR the kitchen) and chatted all night over delicious Persian cuisine. Just before dessert I looked her in the eye and popped the question, ring in hand. And the rest is, as they say... history!
-Noah

Our (second) Family Reunion
Branson, MO
July 5, 2023

FR2.0 was a success! We explored the Titanic museum, went Go-Karting every day, ate too much, and had a great time. Noah and Charlie’s team dominated the Scavenger Hunt, Charlie was a judge at the talent show, and Noah was Top 3 in the guacamole making contest. Success!
-Ashley

FAQs

Answers to hopefully all your burning questions.

  • What is the address for the wedding ceremony and reception venue?

    • Good news! The ceremony and reception will both occur at the Delta Flight Museum located at 1060 Delta Blvd, Hapeville, GA 30354 (about 15 minutes south of downtown Atlanta and right next to the Atlanta Airport). Use that link to open up a Google Map pin with the entrance to the parking lot. If you search the address manually or search for Delta Flight Museum, you will not be take to the parking lot entrance.

  • When does the wedding start and end?

    • The wedding will begin at promptly at 6:00pm EST on Saturday, December 16 (click here for a calendar reminder!).

    Please allow additional time to check-in at the Delta Flight Museum security check point, which may become congested closer to the the event. Also, while it is a Saturday and there aren’t currently any large sporting events, concerts, etc. scheduled in the area, Atlanta traffic can still be bad! Please plan accordingly.

    Last call for the evening is at 10:30pm EST with our send-off shortly thereafter!

  • Will transportation be provided to/from the wedding?

    • No. We will not be offering transportation, but the hotel blocks are all less than a mile from the venue. There is also a very large parking lot on site which is free of charge.

  • VERY IMPORTANT: Where do I park/get dropped off?

    • The entrance to the parking lot is here. Click that link to be open up Google Maps with the exact spot to set your driving directions to... if you search Delta Flight Museum, you will NOT be taken to the proper parking lot. If you find yourself trying to drive through the main Delta HQ security entrance, you have gone the wrong way. Ask the guard for directions.

  • Given that this is a December wedding, can we assume everything is indoors?

    • Yes! All events will be indoors. The Delta Flight Museum has two large hangars within the same building and all events will take place within those two connected hangers.

    There will be a coat check available.

  • Will there be food?

    • If you know us, you know we love to eat! There will be passed appetizers during the cocktail hour and "around the world" food stations for dinner. And of course, we'll have wedding cake and other dessert options!

  • I have dietary restrictions/allergies. What's the best way to let you know?

    • There will be an option to designate any dietary restrictions/allergies with your RSVP and a special meal will be prepared just for you. If you wish to provide any further details, please reach out to the wedding planner, Danielle Taylor, at danielle@bashandbumble.com.

  • VERY IMPORTANT: What should I wear to the wedding?

    • Whatever makes you feel good!

    The dress code will be cocktail attire. For men, this means slacks with a button down and suit jacket. Ties are not required but bonus points if you do wear one and it is travel-themed. There will also be tuxes so feel free to break yours out if you have one in the closet! Please do not come dressed in jeans, shorts, or flip-flops. Fancy tennis shoes are okay!

    For women, short or long cocktail dresses work. Ashley is always happy to see sparkles!

    Need more inspiration? Check out this excellent NYT article on "colorful formal".

  • Can I bring my pets?

    • Noah says no. Sorry!

  • Can I bring my kiddos?

    • Absolutely!

    We will have a dedicated area for kids ages 3 to 11. There will be child care, a kid-friendly food, and fun travel-themed activities!

    The “Unaccompanied Minor” programming will begin with check-in during the cocktail hour and will continue until 8:45pm when the kids will be brought over for cake cutting. While formal programming will end at this time, we will be playing Pixar’s Planes in the flight theater and the babysitters will be in the theater to supervise. Feel free to pack PJs (bonus points for travel-themed!) or let them take a swirl on the dance floor.

    Kids under 3 are invited to attend as "Infant-in-Arms". They will sit next to you during dinner in the adult reception area (aka. the "Delta One" premium cabin). They cannot participate in the Unaccompanied Minor activities, but you can sneak over and make them a plate of kid-friendly foods if you want.

    Important! When RSVP-ing for your kids, you will need to designate either "Unaccompanied Minor" or "Infant-in-Arms" "Unaccompanied Minors" will have a designated seat within the children’s area and "Infant-in-Arms" will have a designated seat within the adult "Delta One" area. Unfortunately, we cannot accommodate seating within both so we thank you in advance for adhering to this!

  • I can’t wait!! When and how do we RSVP?

    • We can’t wait either! You can RSVP starting October 09 (click here for a calendar reminder!) right on this site!

  • Are there any other wedding festivities we should add to the calendar?

    • We will be hosting a welcome party at the Marriot Renaissance Concourse Hotel on Friday, September 15 from 6:00pm - 8:00pm. Heavy hors d'oeuvres will be available with a "capped" open bar (so get there early!).

    There will also be a gentle yoga class at 8:30am on Saturday, December 16 at the Renaissance Concourse Hotel. All are invited (except the groom!). If you are interested in participating, please contact Diane Perry at dianedperry@icloud.com.

    If staying at the Renaissance Concourse Hotel, there will be a hospitality suite available where you can stop by for refreshments throughout the weekend. Consider it your personal "Sky Club"! Please check back for exact details closer to the date!

  • I see there are three hotel blocks. What’s the difference?

    • The Renaissance Concourse Hotel (Marriott) is our preferred hotel. It is the closest hotel to the venue and will be where most of the wedding activities outside of the ceremony/reception will be held.

    The hotel offers king and two queen beds rooms - both with a runway view upgrade option (which we recommend!). Rates range from $149 - $189/night.

    There is an on-site restaurant, coffee shop, fitness center and spa. A free shuttle to/from the airport is available from 4:20am - 12:00am EST. On-site parking is $12/night.

    If you would like to utilize our discounted rate, please use the link below and book no later than November 17.

    Marriot Renaissance Booking Link


    The Kimpton Overland Hotel (IHG) is our recommended hotel for adult travelers. The hotel is next to the Porsche US headquarters and driving experience. It is a modern hotel with European-style rooms (open floor plan bedroom/bathroom).

    The hotel offers king and double beds rooms - though we have only included king rooms in our block. The king room rate is $145/night.

    There is an on-site restaurant, rooftop bar (very fun), fitness center, and indoor pool. There is complimentary wine each evening from 5:00pm - 6:00pm in the lobby. A free shuttle to/from the airport is available from 5:00am - 12:00am EST. Parking is $25/night.

    If you would like to book the discounted rate, please use the link below and book no later than November 15.

    Kimpton Overland Booking Link


    The Hyatt Airport North Hotel (Hyatt) is our recommended hotel for families.

    The hotel offers a king bed (with additional sofa bed) and two double beds (with additional sofa bed) rooms. Rates range from $105 - $110/night.

    There is free breakfast, a fitness center, and on-site 24/7 dining. The hotel is also pet friendly (even if the wedding itself is not). A free shuttle to/from the airport is available from 4:00am - 2:00am EST. Parking is available for free for one vehicle.

    If you would like to book the discounted rate, please use the link below and book no later than November 15.

    Hyatt Place Booking Link


    There is also a Hilton (and several other hotel options) less than a mile from the venue, but we do not have a discounted hotel block rate. As such, you will need to book directly through the hotel.
